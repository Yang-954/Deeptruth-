Project Introduction

DeepTruth: End-to-End Face Forgery Detection System

üîç Problem Statement

With the rapid advancement of AI-generated face technology, deepfake videos and images are becoming increasingly realistic and accessible. This poses significant threats to personal privacy, public trust, and even national security. Traditional detection methods often struggle to keep up with these sophisticated forgeries.

üéØ Our Solution

DeepTruth is an advanced end-to-end face forgery detection system based on the modified Recce framework. By combining RGB pixel data with Frequency Maps, our model can detect subtle artifacts invisible to the human eye, achieving 96.6% accuracy in identifying fake faces.

üåü Key Features

  • üìä Frequency Fusion: Combines RGB and Frequency domains for comprehensive analysis
  • ü§ñ Reconstruction Learning: Uses reconstruction differences to reveal forgeries
  • ‚ö° High Efficiency: Inference time under 100ms for real-time applications
  • üé® User-Friendly Interface: Simple upload-and-detect functionality
